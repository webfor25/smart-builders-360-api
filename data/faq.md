# Smart Builder 360 FAQ and Troubleshooting

This document contains common issues and fixes for Smart Builder 360.

## Not receiving an email (SBCGlobal or Yahoo)
If the contractor has an SBCGlobal or Yahoo email address:
- Prefer using a Gmail account (set it up if needed).
- If staying on Yahoo Mail: add a new contact in Yahoo Mail contacts.
  - Contact email address: no-reply@smartbuilder360.com
  - Use your name as the contact.
- Note: SBCGlobal email forwards to Yahoo.

## Not receiving a SignHere email
If the sender or recipient does not receive a SignHere document for signature:
- Try another email address.
- You will be notified for all future documents to be signed.

## Can't open PDF files via Safari on iOS
- On the iOS device: Settings → search “Safari” → Pop Up Blocker → toggle OFF.
